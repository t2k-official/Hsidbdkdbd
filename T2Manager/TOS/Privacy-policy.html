Privacy Policy

T2Manager

1. Introduction

Welcome to T2M. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you our discord bot, T2Manager.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about your discord account and infomation on the server. The information we may collect on the Site includes:

User Data

This bot may collect your username, user id, the date you joined discord, the date you joined a server and what permissions and roles you have in the server. This data is not stored in the bots databases or kept for longer than 30 days.

Message Data

When you send a message in a server with T2Manager as a member we will collect: When the message was sent, what the message contains and the username that sent the message. This data is used for our automatic Moderation system that will filter out certain words and after checking the content of the message is safe we will then delete all data associated with this message and will not store this data anymore, even if it does trigger the Moderation System the data will still be deleted but all data listed above will be sent as a message in discord to a set logging channel if it violates the discord Tos or the server rules.
